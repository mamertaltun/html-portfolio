The Best Movies According to Mert

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fast and Furious

This is my favourite movie series due to the insane action scenes in each series and the great acting of Vin Diesel.

Organize İşler (Turkish movie)

The director of this comedy is Yılmaz Erdoğan who is the one of the best directors in Turkey. There are plenty of famous comedy actresses in the movie such as Yılmaz Erdoğan, Cem Yılmaz, Demet Akbağ, Tolga Çevik and Ersin Korkut.

Kolpaçino

The diroctor of this comedy is Şafak Sezer. The movie displays the Turkish mafia in an entertaining way.
